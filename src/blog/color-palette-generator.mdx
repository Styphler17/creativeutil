## Features That Make a Difference

*   **Instant Palettes:** Generate a new color palette with a single click.
*   **Harmonious Colors:** Our algorithm ensures that the generated colors work well together.
*   **WCAG Analysis:** We analyze the contrast ratio of your colors to ensure they are accessible to people with visual impairments.
*   **Lock and Adjust:** Lock your favorite colors and generate new ones to complement them.
*   **Export Your Palette:** Copy the hex codes or export your palette as a CSS file.

## How to Use the Color Palette Generator

1.  **Generate a Palette:** Click the "Generate" button to create a new color palette.
2.  **Lock Colors:** Click the lock icon on any color you want to keep.
3.  **Generate Again:** Click "Generate" again to get new colors that work with your locked colors.
4.  **Check Accessibility:** Review the WCAG analysis to ensure your colors are accessible.
5.  **Export Your Palette:** Copy the hex codes or download the CSS file.
